Money Counter — free online cash, coin & currency counter

Free online money counter — count cash, coins and any currency

A free money counter and cash counter for the web. Count currency notes and coins fast for USD, EUR, GBP, INR, AED, or build your own custom currency with any denominations. Note Counter is free, open-source under the MIT license, and works offline as a Progressive Web App. All data stays on your device.

Key features

  • Multi-currency counter for notes and coins (INR, USD, EUR, GBP, AED)
  • Unlimited custom currencies with your own code, symbol, and denominations
  • Smart input — type math expressions like +50, -20, 10*5
  • Built-in calculator with history
  • Save sessions with hash references (#1, #2 …) and export as PDF
  • Privacy mode, PIN / password lock, comprehensive keyboard shortcuts
  • Installable PWA — works fully offline after first load
  • All data stored locally in your browser; nothing about your amounts is uploaded
