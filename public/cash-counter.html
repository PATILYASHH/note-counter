The cash counter your till deserves

Cash counting machines are fast, but they cost hundreds of dollars and they break. This cash counter is a free web app that does the same job for low and medium cash volumes — perfect for small shops, market traders, food trucks, salons, tutors, fundraisers and home use.

Here is the workflow: bundle your bills by denomination, count each pile, type the count into the matching field, and watch the running total. Save the session, export a PDF, and you have a tidy cash-up sheet for your books.

Cash counting workflow

  1. Pick your currency (USD, EUR, GBP, INR, AED, or a custom one).
  2. Sort the cash into piles by denomination.
  3. Count each pile and type the number into the field — typos can be corrected with math, e.g. +5 or -2.
  4. Read the running total at the top.
  5. Save the session with a hash reference (#1, #2…) and export a PDF if you need a record.

Cash counter FAQ

How accurate is it?

The arithmetic is exact. The accuracy depends on you counting the bundles correctly — the tool just removes the multiplication step.

Can I track multiple tills in one session?

Yes — save each till as its own session with a hash reference (#1, #2…) and export each one separately.

Will my cash totals leave my device?

No. All counts are stored in your browser's localStorage. Only anonymous page-view counts (no amounts) are tracked through Vercel Analytics.

Is there an Android or iOS app?

The web app installs directly as a PWA — open the site on your phone, choose "Add to Home Screen," and it behaves like a native app, fully offline.
