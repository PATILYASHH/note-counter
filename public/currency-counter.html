One counter, every currency

Most online currency counters are locked to one country. This one is not. Switch between USD, EUR, GBP, INR and AED with a tap, or build your own currency with any name, symbol and denomination set. Useful for travellers totalling leftover foreign cash, NGOs working across borders, and shops near tourist areas accepting more than one currency.

Why a multi-currency counter

  • Travellers — total leftover foreign cash before exchanging or your next trip.
  • Tourist-area businesses — accept multiple currencies and reconcile each separately.
  • Forex booths — count each tray of cash by currency, save sessions per shift.
  • NGOs & aid workers — track local currency receipts in the field, no internet required.
  • Cross-border traders — keep a clean PDF record per currency for accounting.
  • Collectors — total face value of mixed-currency stamp or coin holdings.

Make it your currency

The custom currency feature lets you do things a generic counter cannot:

  • Old pre-decimal British coins (with face values you set).
  • Casino chips by colour and value.
  • Transport tokens or arcade tokens.
  • Voucher books with fixed-value coupons.
  • Any local-shop loyalty currency or scrip.

Once a custom currency is created it appears alongside the built-ins, with the same total, save and PDF-export features.

Currency counter FAQ

How many currencies can I create?

Unlimited custom currencies, each with unlimited denominations.

Will custom currencies sync between devices?

No — everything is stored locally for privacy. To move data, use the JSON export/import feature.

Does it know exchange rates?

No. The counter does not perform conversion — it tells you the face-value total in each currency you count.

Can I share a custom currency with a colleague?

Yes — export your data as JSON and they import it on their device. No account, no cloud.
