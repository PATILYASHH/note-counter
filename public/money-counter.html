What this money counter does

This is a free online money counter that adds up the value of physical cash for you. Whether you are doing end-of-day cash-up at a shop, splitting a tip jar, balancing a till, or just curious how much is in your wallet, the tool turns counting money into a 30-second job.

You enter how many notes and coins you have of each denomination, and the running total updates as you type. You can also use math expressions like +50, -20 or 10*5 directly in any field — no calculator-switching needed.

Money counter FAQ

What is a money counter?

It is any tool that totals the value of physical cash by multiplying each denomination by how many you have. This one runs in your web browser, so no machine or download is needed.

Is it really free?

Yes — every feature is free, there are no ads, and the source is on GitHub. If it saves you time you can optionally sponsor the project.

Does it work without the internet?

Yes. After the first load the app is cached and works offline as a PWA. Install it to your phone or desktop home screen for one-tap access.

Where is my data stored?

In your browser only (localStorage). Nothing about the amounts you count is uploaded to any server.
