## 📊 **Privacy & Data Handling**

### **What We Collect (via Google AdSense)**
- 🍪 **Cookies**: Advertising cookies for personalized ads and analytics
- 📊 **Analytics**: Anonymous usage data (page views, interactions)
- 🎯 **Ad Data**: Ad impressions, clicks, and performance metrics
- 📱 **Device Info**: Browser type, OS, screen size for ad targeting
- 🌍 **Location**: Approximate location (country/region) for relevant ads

### **What Stays Private (Local Storage Only)**
- ✅ **Counting Numbers**: Your actual money amounts never leave your device
- ✅ **Counting History**: All saved sessions stored locally
- ✅ **Quick Notes**: Your notepad content stays on your device
- ✅ **Calculator History**: Calculation records remain local
- ✅ **App Settings**: Preferences stored in browser only
- ✅ **Web Lock PIN**: Security codes never transmitted
- ✅ **Custom Currencies**: Your custom currency designs stay local

### **User Privacy Controls**
- 🔐 **Web Lock**: PIN/password protection for your data
- 👁️ **Privacy Mode**: Hide amounts with toggle (Ctrl+H)
- 📤 **Data Export**: Download all your data anytime
- 🗑️ **Data Deletion**: Clear data via browser settings or app reset
- 🍪 **Cookie Settings**: Manage preferences via [Google Ad Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads)
- 🚫 **Opt-Out**: Disable personalized ads through cookie banner
